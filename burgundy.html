A long time coming

We met one grey October at a friend's gallery opening, where Theodore was quietly convinced the sculpture was hung upside down and Eleanor was quietly certain he was wrong. Two hours and a great deal of terrible wine later, neither of us had conceded the point — and neither of us wanted the evening to end.

Seven years, three cities, and one very opinionated spaniel later, we are still arguing about that sculpture. We would be honoured to have you with us as we make it official on the shores of Lake Como.

Making a weekend of it

Travel & Stay

Como is loveliest in June. We have gathered a few notes to make the journey simple — from the nearest airports to the hotels holding rooms in our name.

I.

Getting There

Fly into Milan Malpensa (MXP), one hour by car. Private transfers can be arranged on request.

II.

Where to Stay

Room blocks held at the Grand Hotel Tremezzo and Villa d'Este. Mention the Ashford–Whitmore wedding.

III.

Things to Do

Boat the lake, wander Bellagio's gardens, or simply take a long lunch. We recommend all three.

Everything else

Questions

What should I wear?

Black tie — long dresses and dinner jackets. The ceremony is on the lawn, so heels and soft grass may need reconsidering.

May I bring a plus-one?

Your invitation will name everyone we have reserved a seat for. If you have a question about your party, do write to us.

Are children welcome?

We adore your little ones, but this will be an adults-only celebration — a proper night off for the parents among us.

Is there parking at the villa?

Valet parking is available at the gate. We do encourage the shuttle from the hotels — the lake road is narrow and the wine is good.
